The Struggle for Freedom in La Española
Editorial, Book Design
Visual Resistance (collaborative) | Fall 2021

A documentation book on an animated and web-based narrative commemorating the 500th anniversary of the first African slave revolt in the Americas. The book catalogs the process of creating the motion graphics, animations, and illustrations. My role in the Book Design team included details such as the illustrated book cover, map, and moon phases, as well as making typographic choices, designing chapter layouts, and curating the final draft.
