Peekskill: A Friendly Town
Branding, Digital Design
Digital Design Portfolio | Fall 2021

Reimagining a visualization of the revitalized city of Peekskill. The city currently offers the experience of vibrant art, music, and downtown restaurant scenes that have attracted a surge of visitors. Through a redesigned logo, posters, and postcards featuring iconic landmarks of the city, this project captures the essence of Peekskill's new era, inviting viewers to explore, indulge, and fall in love with this friendly town.
