The Life of Princess Diana
Digital Design
Web Design II | Spring 2021

Created a website using Bootstrap Studio, designed in magazine style, to chronicle the life of Princess Diana. Featuring immersive layouts, matching imagery, and interactive elements, the website offers a comprehensive exploration of Princess Diana's journey, including her philanthropic work, important fashion moments, and memorable milestones. The responsive design ensures seamless viewing across devices, allowing users to delve into the captivating world of a global icon.
