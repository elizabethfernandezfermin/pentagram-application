Hi! My name is Elizabeth Fernandez Fermin (or simply Liz). I am a Dominican-American designer, coder, and art lover.

I was born in La Vega, Dominican Republic, and immigrated to the small town of Peekskill, New York as a child. The experiences I have lived have given me a unique perspective of the world that has allowed me to be empathic to the different paths everyone takes and open to change and new ideas.

I graduated from the City College of New York with a B.A. degree with a concentration in Digital Design in 2022. I am always looking for opportunities to grow as a designer and as a person.
