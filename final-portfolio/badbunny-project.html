bad bunny interview
graphic design

Made for my Electronic Design 1 class, this magazine chapter is an interview with Latin Reggaeton artist Bad Bunny. For the project, we were tasked with creating a 3-4 spread magazine chapter about a topic of our choice, including a full-page cover. The actual interview was done by Carina del Valle Schorske for the New York Times, published in October of 2020. Since the interview had to be in a question-answer format, I included parts of the interview that I thought were important and created questions for them.

software:

Indesign, Photoshop.

The topics that seemed as most important from the interview included the influences that Bad Bunny had growing up, the impact of Hurricane Maria on his homeland Puerto Rico, and social issues such as racism and corruption that he had been very vocal about at the time. The images are credited on the page they are displayed in, coming from different covers and/or performances that Bad Bunny has been in. For the typeface, I chose Gadugi, using it in bold for the titles and subtitles, and regular for the body copy.

In terms of color, I decided to use a shade of orange as the accent color throughout the magazine, including it in background rectangular shapes, frames, questions, and image captions. Another design decision was to use an overlapping element using the images, frames and background shapes to give the interview a more dynamic feeling.
