separated by the ocean
web design

This project was given in my Web Design 2 class, where we were tasked with writing a story that pertained to us personally and included events and/or a timeline of some sort. I chose to write about the different experiences and feelings Iâ€™ve had between where I was born, Dominican Republic, and where I live now, New York. This is emphasized with the story starting and ending with both places named side to side. As a scrolling website you go from a sharp red to blue, going through the story itself. Some parts were designed using animations, while in others I focused on the typographical element of the words and letters.

software:

HTML, CSS

visit the website!

Since the story is structured with contrasting statements, the website experience is like this as well. I emphasize the different feeling each phrase gives through the change of color for the words, adding borders to sections and animation of words such as rotation and sliding. Depending on what the words convey, they are placed close apart or separated significantly.

In terms of my color palette, I stuck to shades of blue and red, as well as adding shades of green and yellow to certain words to emphasize what they were to convey. In terms of fonts, I used a variety of them in order to differentiate the feeling of the words. The typefaces used include Monserrat, Nosifer, Henny Penny, and Carter One.
